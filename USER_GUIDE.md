## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. **Sign Up:**
   - Visit the registration page
   - Enter your email and username
   - Create a secure password

2. **Trial Access:**
   - New members get 7-day trial access
   - Full access to chat features
   - 50 messages per month during trial

3. **Login:**
   - Use your username and password
   - Or use SSO from your membership platform

### First Steps

1. **Explore the Chat:**
   - Start with a simple question
   - Ask about hair education or business mentorship
   - Get familiar with the interface

2. **Check Your Dashboard:**
   - View your usage statistics
   - See your membership tier
   - Track your trial period

## Using the Chat

### Sending Messages

1. **Type Your Question:**
   - Click in the message input
   - Type your question
   - Press Enter or click Send

2. **Message Guidelines:**
   - Be specific with your questions
   - Provide context when needed
   - Ask follow-up questions

3. **Message Limits:**
   - Maximum 4000 characters per message
   - Messages are validated for security
   - Empty messages are not allowed

### Understanding Responses

**Response Features:**
- **Markdown Formatting:** Responses use markdown for better readability
- **Source Citations:** Sources are shown for knowledge-based answers
- **Real-time Streaming:** Responses stream in real-time
- **Context Awareness:** AI remembers conversation history

**Response Types:**
- **Hair Education:** Questions about hair care, styling, maintenance
- **Business Mentorship:** Questions about business strategy, pricing, marketing
- **Product Recommendations:** Suggestions for products and tools
- **Troubleshooting:** Help with problems and challenges

### Conversation History

**View History:**
- All conversations are saved
- Access via chat history
- Search through past conversations

**Clear History:**
- Option to clear chat history
- Useful for starting fresh
- Does not affect usage limits

## Understanding Your Usage

### Usage Dashboard

**View Your Dashboard:**
- Navigate to `/dashboard`
- See your usage statistics
- Track your limits

**Dashboard Shows:**
- Messages used vs. limit
- Tokens consumed
- API costs (if applicable)
- Trial status (for Basic tier)
- Days remaining in trial

### Usage Limits

**Basic Tier (Trial):**
- 50 messages per month
- 7-day trial period
- Resets on 1st of month

**VIP Tier (Elite):**
- 1000 messages per month
- Full access
- No trial restrictions

### Usage Tracking

**What's Tracked:**
- Messages sent
- Tokens consumed (input + output)
- API costs (for admin visibility)

**When Limits Reset:**
- Monthly limits reset on the 1st of each month
- Trial periods are one-time (7 days from signup)

### Usage Limit Exceeded

**What Happens:**
- You'll see a message limit notification
- Upgrade prompt will appear
- Previous messages remain accessible

**How to Upgrade:**
- Click upgrade link
- Follow membership platform instructions
- Access restored immediately after upgrade

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Can't Send Messages

**Possible Causes:**
- Usage limit reached
- Trial period expired
- Rate limit exceeded

**Solutions:**
- Check your usage dashboard
- Upgrade your membership
- Wait a few minutes and try again

#### Slow Responses

**Possible Causes:**
- High system load
- Network issues
- Complex queries

**Solutions:**
- Wait for response to complete
- Check your internet connection
- Simplify your question

#### Messages Not Saving

**Possible Causes:**
- Browser storage issues
- Network connectivity
- Session expired

**Solutions:**
- Refresh the page
- Check your connection
- Login again if needed

## FAQ

### How do I upgrade my membership?

Click the upgrade link when you see the usage limit message, or visit your membership platform to upgrade.

### Can I extend my trial?

Trials are one-time 7-day periods. Upgrade to Elite for full access.

### What happens to my messages when I hit the limit?

Your previous messages remain accessible. You just can't send new messages until the limit resets or you upgrade.

### How do I clear my chat history?

Use the "Clear History" option in the chat interface. This doesn't affect your usage limits.

### Can I use TayAI on mobile?

Yes! The chat interface is fully responsive and works on mobile devices.

### How accurate are the responses?

Responses are based on the TaysLuxe knowledge base and GPT-4. They're designed to be accurate and helpful, but always use your judgment.
